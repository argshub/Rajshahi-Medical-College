Welcome to Rajshahi Medical College & Hospital

Hello everybody, Welcome to the official webpage of Rajshahi Medical College & Hospital. This is one of the A Grade Govt. Medical College in Bangladesh situated at Rajshahi which is very nearer to Rajshahi city. It has very superb well built college and hospital buildings on its own land of about 4 acres. The College started with its first admission of 50 students from the academic session 2003-2004. Now the admission intake is 140 per year. The 750 bedded running Hospital has outdoor & indoor facilities and always busy with patients. Academic faculty is very enriched with senior renowned Professors and also energetic dedicated junior and mid level teachers. Very good Hostel Facilities for both local and overseas students are available. Library with all recommended Text books, Reference books, Journals including International ones are provided. Internet facilities including International online linkage HINARI which can provide up to date information to students and teachers. Extra academic cultural activities, games and sports facilities are there. Twenty five percent (25%) seats are available for foreign students. The medium of instruction is in English. The College is also included in the list of international directory of Medical Schools like IMED, FAIMER and AVISINA.
